Get Donor

Your personal Well - Wisher

Get Donor is a website which you will need the most when you get into any accident(God forbid).This Web-app shows you the nearest locations in which donors are available with their blood groups and will also alert one of your family member. In the future scope of the project we also aim to add an alert system in our project which will alert the near by doctors in that particular area about the emergency.
